    \section{CTQ 1}
For a roll of a pair of fair 6-sided dice, the sample space is defined as:
\begin{equation}
\Omega = \{(i,j) \,|\, 1\leq i,j\leq 6\}
\end{equation}
and we have equiprobability.

Then, the probability of any event equals the number of favorable cases divided by the number of possible cases, which is:
\begin{equation}
card(\Omega) = 6 \times 6 = 36
\end{equation}

f\colon \mathbb{R}^m & \longrightarrow\mathbb{R}^n\\
